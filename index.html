The free acne-trigger scanner app

Welcome

AcnErase is a free educational tool that empowers users to scan ingredient labels on foods and skincare products. It helps identify ingredients that may trigger acne, based on years of personal research.

Why Use AcnErase?

This app was built for anyone struggling with acne and seeking answers. If you've ever felt food might be contributing to your skin problems, AcnErase helps you identify the culprits — without gimmicks, ads, or invasive data collection.

Important: What to Expect

Using AcnErase won't produce overnight results if you continue eating the same diet. For best outcomes, follow the Purging Diet while strictly avoiding the ingredients the app flags.

Neu5Gc, a molecule from animal foods, can remain in the bloodstream and tissues. Initial improvement may come within a few weeks, but new acne may still appear until blood levels drop significantly. Once purging begins, some users report dramatic changes in complexion — and even shifts in sense of smell, especially toward dairy.

Trust the process. Stick with it. Let your body flush the triggers out.

Welcome to AcnErase

Your free app to identify acne triggers in food and skincare

About AcnErase

Created by Martin Hamer, a former nurse and long-time acne sufferer, AcnErase is based on over 22 years of personal research. It empowers users with a scanner and ingredient database to make better-informed choices for their skin.

How It Works

Simply scan a label. AcnErase highlights known acne-related triggers, including hidden dairy and gluten-based ingredients. The app is free, with no ads or sales pitches — just a tool to help you take control of your skin and confidence.

Privacy First

We don't collect or share any personal data. AcnErase works entirely offline to protect your privacy.

Why Immediate Results May Take Time

If you’ve just started using AcnErase, please don’t be discouraged if breakouts continue for a week or two. The app helps you avoid common acne-triggering ingredients, but real change begins when your body has time to adjust.

Our 22+ years of research suggest that many people — especially those consuming Neu5Gc-rich foods (like dairy and red meat) — need to follow a short-term “purging diet” to clear built-up triggers from their system. During this time, even a small amount of Neu5Gc can still cause breakouts until levels in the bloodstream drop significantly.

During this “purging” period, many users report:

  • A sudden, noticeable improvement in complexion
  • A dramatic shift in sense of smell (foods like cheese may begin to smell off-putting)
  • Renewed awareness of how food affects their skin

Healing takes time. If you commit to avoiding the ingredients flagged by the app and follow a clean elimination diet, you’ll begin to see — and feel — the difference. We’re here to support you through it.

About AcnErase

AcnErase is a free, ad-free educational app developed to help users identify acne-triggering ingredients in foods and skincare labels—especially those containing Neu5Gc, dairy, and gluten.

Created by Martin Hamer, a former nurse and long-time acne sufferer, AcnErase is based on over 22 years of personal research. It empowers users with a scanner and ingredient database to make better-informed choices for their skin.

How It Works

Simply scan a label. AcnErase highlights known acne-related triggers, including hidden dairy and gluten-based ingredients. For best results, consult your healthcare provider before making changes to your diet.

Privacy First

We don't collect or share any personal data. AcnErase works entirely offline to protect your privacy.

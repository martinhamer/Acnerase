Your free app to identify acne triggers in food and skincare

About AcnErase

Developed by a former nurse and acne sufferer with 70 years’ experience, AcnErase helps you identify hidden acne triggers like Neu5Gc, dairy, and gluten in foods and skincare products. The app is free, with no ads or sales pitches — just a tool to help you take control of your skin and confidence.
